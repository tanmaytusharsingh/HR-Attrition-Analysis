# HR-Attrition-Analysis
Project Overview:-

This project analyzes employee attrition data to understand why employees leave an organization. The dashboard provides clear insights into attrition patterns based on factors like age, salary, job role, and experience.

The goal of this project is to help HR teams identify high-risk areas and take actions to improve employee retention.

Objectives:-
- Analyze employee attrition trends
- Identify key factors affecting attrition
- Understand employee demographics and behavior
- Support data-driven HR decision-making
  
Tools Used:-
- Microsoft Excel 
- Power BI
  
Dataset Features:-
- Employee Count
- Attrition Count
- Age
- Salary
- Job Role
- Education Field
- Years at Company
- Monthly Income

Dashboard Features:-
- Attrition by Education Field
- Attrition by Age Group
- Attrition by Salary Slab
- Attrition by Years at Company
- Attrition by Job Role
  
Key Insights:-
- Highest attrition is seen in the 26–35 age group
- Employees with lower salary show higher attrition
- Certain job roles have significantly higher attrition
- Most attrition happens in the early years of employment
- Education field also impacts attrition patterns
